Recording Nerve Signals (Model)

In Python, I've modeled extracellular neural recordings from implanted electrodes. By integrating geometric weighting and membrane current derivatives, this simulation accurately reconstructs the extracellular potential detected at the electrode interface. This tool provides a framework for optimizing electrode design/placement and signal processing in applications such as functional electrical stimulation (FES) and in-vivo nerve monitoring.
