Electrical Modeling of Neuron & Propagation of Action Potential

In Python, I've computationally modeled the Hodgkin-Huxley model the neuron as an electrical circuit. Utilizing this model, we can simulate how different stimuli (such as via an implanted electrode) can excite neurons in a variety of conditions. This model accounts for membrane resistance & capacitance, axial resistance, source current, ion channels, and myelination.
