Full Basic Microprocessor with MIPS Assembly Game

Designed a full basic microprocessor in SystemVerilog and implemented it on a Nexys A7 circuit development platform. Then, developed a complex MIPS assembly game that performs bitwise operations on the microprocessor to dynamically write to audio output, a 7-segment display, and a VGA monitor based on input from a keyboard and accelerometer.
